# Product Vision

## What is CareerKete?

CareerKete helps young New Zealanders (rangatahi, ages 14-25) explore career possibilities through friendly, AI-powered conversations. It's the opposite of a boring career quiz — it's a warm chat with a curious friend who listens first, then shows you paths that might click.

## Two products, one platform

**CareerKete (individual/consumer)**: Free tier for any rangatahi. Chat, browse 115+ NZ careers, save favourites, build action plans with AI-powered steps.

**CareerKete for Schools (B2B)**: Paid platform for secondary schools. Adds counsellor dashboard, collaborative plans, student engagement insights, and reporting. Students remain in control of their data — schools get permissioned access, not ownership.

## Core principle: user sovereignty

Users own their data, always. Linking to a school is opt-in and reversible. Students control what's shared with counsellors via granular permission toggles. Leaving a school keeps all personal content intact; counsellors retain their own professional records (notes) separately.

## Target audiences

- **Students**: ages 14-25, any NZ rangatahi figuring out "what's next"
- **Parents/whānau**: support their teen's exploration without pressure
- **Guidance counsellors**: overwhelmed with caseloads, need better tools
- **School leaders**: want data on engagement and outcomes

## Values

- Warm, non-judgmental, never preachy
- All career paths valid: trade, university, self-employment, apprenticeship
- Te Reo woven in naturally
- Built in Aotearoa, for Aotearoa

## Key features

**For students**:
- AI chat that listens first
- 115+ NZ careers across 11 categories
- Save careers, build action plans
- AI agent that takes real actions (drafts emails, finds providers, writes CVs)
- Syncs across all devices

**For schools (v2 Phase 2+)**:
- Counsellor dashboard showing their students
- Engagement insights (who's active, who's disengaged)
- Collaborative action plans (co-edited with students)
- Private counsellor notes
- Messaging between counsellor and student
- Reporting for board, ERO, MOE

## Timeline

- **Q4 2026**: v2 solo launch (public)
- **Q1 2027**: v2 schools launch (pilot)
- **Term 1 2027**: First 5 pilot schools go live

## Commercial model

- **Consumer**: Free forever, all core features
- **Schools**: Tiered pricing ($4,500 - $10,800/year depending on size)
- **Parent-direct (optional)**: $49/year for premium features outside school channels
- **Equity pricing**: 50% discount for decile 1-3 schools
